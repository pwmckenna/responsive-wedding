CAITLIN

Caitlin grew up in Cypress, California with a wonderful sprawling family. They're a caring, protective (uh hem), and exciting bunch, and she's heartbreakingly happy when they're near. I thought meeting Caitlin meant the end of online profiles. WRONG.
